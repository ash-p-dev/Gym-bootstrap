Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What are your gym hours?
Our gym is open from Monday to Friday, 5 AM to 10 PM, and on weekends from 7 AM to 8 PM.

Where are you located?
We are located at 123 Fitness Lane, Your City, Your State, ZIP Code.

Do I need to make an appointment to visit the gym?
No, appointments are not necessary for general gym access. However, if you want to book a personal training session or join a class, we recommend reserving a spot in advance.

Membership Questions

What types of memberships do you offer?
We offer several membership options including Basic, Standard, and Premium plans. Each plan has different benefits and pricing. You can find more details on our Membership page.

Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for a nominal fee. Please contact our membership services for more details and to initiate the freeze.

How do I cancel my membership?
To cancel your membership, please visit the front desk or contact our customer service team. We require a 30-day notice period for cancellations.

Classes and Training

What types of classes do you offer?
We offer a variety of group fitness classes including yoga, spinning, Zumba, and strength training. Check our Class Schedule for details and availability.

Do I need to book a spot in advance for classes?
Yes, we recommend booking your spot in advance to secure your place. You can book classes online through our website or at the gym.

Do you offer personal training sessions?
Yes, we offer personal training sessions with certified trainers. You can book sessions through our website or speak with a trainer at the gym.

Equipment and Facilities

What types of equipment do you have?
Our gym is equipped with a wide range of equipment including treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, free weights, resistance machines, and more. See our Equipment page for a detailed list.

Are towels provided?
We provide towels for use during your workout. You can also bring your own towel if you prefer.

Do you have showers and lockers?
Yes, we have shower facilities and lockers available for use. Please bring your own lock for the lockers or purchase one at the front desk.

Safety and Health

What measures are in place for gym cleanliness and hygiene?
We prioritize cleanliness and have regular cleaning schedules. Sanitizing stations are available throughout the gym, and we encourage all members to clean equipment before and after use.

What should I do if I’m feeling unwell?
If you’re feeling unwell, please refrain from coming to the gym. If you have specific health concerns or conditions, consult with your healthcare provider before resuming exercise.

Do you have staff available for medical emergencies?
Our staff are trained in basic first aid and CPR. In case of a medical emergency, please alert a staff member immediately.

Membership Fees and Payments

What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept various forms of payment including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. Monthly membership fees are billed automatically to your payment method.

Are there any additional fees?
There may be additional fees for certain services, such as personal training sessions or classes that are not included in your membership plan. Please check our Membership Terms for full details.

Contact Information

How can I contact you for further questions?
You can contact us via phone at (123) 456-7890, email at info@flickergyms.com, or visit us at the gym during operating hours.
