Our Gym Equipment

Explore the wide range of high-quality gym equipment available at Flicker Gyms to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Treadmill

Our treadmills offer a smooth running experience with various speed settings and incline options to suit all fitness levels.

Elliptical Trainer

Designed to provide a low-impact cardio workout, our ellipticals come with adjustable resistance and pre-set programs.

Dumbbells

Our selection of dumbbells ranges from light to heavy, suitable for strength training and muscle building.

Stationary Bike

Provides a great cardiovascular workout while minimizing impact on joints. Features adjustable resistance and various pre-set programs.

Rowing Machine

Offers a full-body workout that combines cardio and strength training. Adjustable resistance levels and various workout modes available.

Lat Pulldown Machine

Targets the upper back and lats with adjustable weight and grip positions for a variety of exercises.

Glute Machine

Focuses on strengthening and toning the glute muscles with adjustable weights and various exercise options.

Kettlebells

Offers a range of weights for dynamic strength training exercises like swings, snatches, and goblet squats.

Battle Ropes

Provides a high-intensity workout for improving strength, endurance, and cardiovascular fitness.

Yoga Mats

Essential for yoga, stretching, and floor exercises, providing cushioning and grip.

Resistance Bands

Versatile tools for strength training, stretching, and rehabilitation, available in various resistance levels.
